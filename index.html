Biography

I am a Compiler, JIT compiler, Dynamic Binary Translator, and Deep Learning researcher, with a bachelor in Computer Science (UEM, 2016) and on the way to finish a doctorate in Computer Science in one of the most prestigious universities from Latin America, UNICAMP (SP/Brazil). During the last years, I also had experience with internships in the industry: MATERA Systems in 2010, Samsung Brasil in 2017 and Microsoft Research in 2019. Moreover, I had the opportunity to do part of my doctorate as invited research at INESC-ID in Lisbon/Portugal working with deep learning deployment, optimization, and hardware co-design.

I have a wide range of programming and software engineering skills with a long term experience working with modern C++, C, RISC-V and x86_64 Assembly, Python, and GNU/Linux systems.
